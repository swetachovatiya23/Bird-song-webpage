Rooster Bird

Males (called cocks or roosters) and females (hens) are known for their fleshy combs, lobed wattles hanging below the bill, and high-arched tails. In some roosters, the tail can extend more than 30 cm (12 inches) in length. Cockfighting was instrumental in bringing about the selection of these birds for larger size.
